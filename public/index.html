Fixed Assets

We are pleased to announce that the management and tracking of all Fixed Assets has been migrated from Snipe-IT to Follett Destiny which has previously been utilized for library management.

Follett Destiny provides enhanced tracking capabilities as well as reporting and inventory control for all asset tracking needs. The new system also streamlines the approval and processing of surplus assets by saving time and increasing efficiency.
